Clinical Performance Update: Comparative Benchmark Analysis

AUC stands for Area Under the Curve and is a numerical representation of sensitivity versus specificity. 

These improvements confirm that our model is on track for an AUC of >0.8 as we continue development through Phase III. To contextualize this score, the FDA target is 0.7, and 0.628 already represents an improvement against the current gold standard for diagnosis, the CAPS-5 along with other clinician measures administeq12red via long-form clinical interviews. While we have seen higher AUC scores/accuracy this is our most robust model and we suspect this will be our best- the one we submit to FDA. It has only been trained on a subset of our data, and will continue to improve.
